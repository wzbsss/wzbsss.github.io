有机咖啡（Organic Coffee）

它是咖啡树生长过程中，无化学原料、不用农药、杀虫剂、除草剂及人工食物添加剂种植的；从土壤水质检测、种植、培育、采收、生产都必须符合有机的条件、过程严格控管，是与大自然和谐共存下生产出来的高品质咖啡，对健康及环保都有很大的益处。
It is the coffee trees grow in the process, no chemical raw material, no pesticides, insecticides, herbicides, and artificial food additives plant; From soil water quality detection, planting, cultivation, harvesting, production must conform to the conditions of the organic, strict process control, is under the harmonious coexistence with nature to produce high quality coffee, is of great benefit to health and environmental protection are.

卡布奇诺(Cappuccino)

卡布奇诺是意大利咖啡文化的主流。名称由来是：在意大利人眼中看来，在热牛奶和浓咖啡的混合上的牛奶帽盖，很像教堂僧侣所穿戴的连帽长袍。 而卡布奇诺最令人陶醉的便是那细致温暖的牛奶泡沬，温柔的包裹着咖啡的热度，让你回味无穷。
Cappuccino is an Italian coffee culture of the mainstream.Name origin is, in the eyes of the italians, it seems, on a mixture of hot milk and espresso milk cap, like monks in the church of the hooded robes.And cappuccino intoxicating is the most meticulous warm milk bubble, the heat of the gentle parcel coffee, let you aftertaste endless.

把热咖啡、砂糖、红葡萄酒倒入小锅中加热，再徐徐倒入杯中，咖啡上加一片柠檬和肉桂棒，肉桂棒的作用就等于汤匙，在轻轻搅拌兼，随热气飘散出来的，除了咖啡香之外，还有肉桂和柠檬的芬芳。
The hot coffee, sugar, red wine in small pot heating, and then pour cup, coffee and a slice of lemon and cinnamon stick, the effect of cinnamon stick is equal to the spoon, in and stir gently, as heat spill out, in addition to coffee, cinnamon and lemon scent.
